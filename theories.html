How Did Humans Aquire Language?

One of the most debated topics among linguists is the origins of language. Most linguists also consider it the most mysterious topic because no one knows how to explain how language emerged and how humans were able to adapt to it and use it efficiently.

There exists many theories claiming to have figured out how humans were able to evolve such a complex and rich language.
